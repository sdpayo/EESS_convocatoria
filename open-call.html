Open Call - Espacios Sonoros 2026

International open call with no restrictions

Espacios Sonoros is pleased to announce a new open call for the submission of multichannel electroacoustic works. The 2026 edition of the festival will take place in Salta, Argentina, in September.

The 2026 call will have two categories: an international open call with no restrictions and a call for young Latin American composers (up to 35 years old).

Both calls will be open until June 23. The jury will announce the selected works in mid-July. Works of up to 15 minutes in duration will be accepted, preferably in B-format ambisonics. Each composer may submit only one work. There is no fee for submitting to the call or for participating in the concert. The presence of the composers at the concert is desirable but not required.

This form corresponds to the international open call with no restrictions.
