Open Call - Espacios Sonoros 2026

International open call without restrictions

Espacios Sonoros is pleased to announce its open call for multichannel electroacoustic works. The 2026 edition of the festival will be held in Salta (Argentina) in September.

This year's Open Call will have two categories: an international open call without restrictions and an open call directed at young latin american composers (under 35 years old).

Submissions will be received until June 23rd. The selected works will be announced in July. Submitted works should be between 5 and 15 minutes long. Ambisonics B-format is preferred, but not required. There will be no submission or registration fees. The composers' presence at the 2026 concerts is encouraged but not required.

This form is for the general open call.

Espacios Sonoros is free for composers and audience, please consider making a donation through PayPal or Mercado Pago. Donations will be used for press and staging.
